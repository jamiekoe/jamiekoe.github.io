Research

Translating multimodal data into deployable biomarkers.

01

Mobile Eye Tracking for Parkinson's Disease

Developed and validated an iPad-based eye-movement assessment platform against clinical-grade eye tracking across pro-saccade, anti-saccade, memory-guided, and self-generated saccade tasks. Current work builds synchronized EyeLink-iPad datasets, saccade-specific gaze models for Parkinson's screening, and neural-rendered synthetic data for more robust tablet-based gaze estimation.

02

Pediatric Concussion Biomarkers

Extended mobile oculomotor assessment to pediatric concussion research in 90 children, identifying feasible age-adjusted biomarkers and clarifying which task-derived measures are robust in young children.

03

Physiological Monitoring in VR

Processed ECG, PPG, and behavioral performance data from virtual-reality flight tasks, showing that heart-rate trends track workload and performance in safety-critical training settings.

04

Machine Learning for Neural Engineering

Built patient-specific early seizure detection methods, open-source EEG preprocessing software for seizure-centered data windows, GPU-parallelized genetic algorithms, and hardware-aware model selection for neuromodulation devices.
